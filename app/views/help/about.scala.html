The colour ranking is based on the idea that no one knows better the quality of a journal or a conference than the members of its research community. Our objective is to gather this information through votes in order to provide an accurate score regarding the quality of a publication

Scoring method

To be as exhaustive as possible, the scores are based on two sources:

  • User votes
  • Scores from external ranking, e.g., Core, Microsoft Academic Research or Google Scholar

You can get detailed information on how the scores are computed from these different sources by visiting the sections User scores and External scores.

Authors disclaimer on research evaluation

The authors strongly believe that a research evaluation based only on the ranking of the journals or conferences leads to bad practices. However, it remains a good indicator and as such we hope that the colour ranking will be helpful.
